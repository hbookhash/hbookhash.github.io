About Us

We are a startup company aiming to provide solutions for digitlization, environment and energy conservation.

Digitlization in various social fields will help in various ways

Good Connectivity

Conneting more peoples leads to share of more ideas and good use of resources

Efficient Solution

With emarging new technologies like Machine Learning, various solutions can be made which will increase efficiency and profits based on needs.

Common Work

Providing similar solutions for all having similar needs will save a lot of resourses and hence will be pocket friendly

Keeping all the above factors in mind, various needs were searched for.

Amazingly indeed various solutions already exists, however it is needed for it to reach to correct peoples, regarding this fact, farmers and many occupations having low income have been away from it.

Even if the case is not for money, because of tradition and danger from getting away from old faison and easy jobs (which are neither easy nor is profitable in most cases) existing solutions too are not utilized. As a result, still polybags are in use, food is adulterated shopkeeprs have to spend their whole day just for providing goods with can be made automatic.

So along with need we also realized, there shoud be proper planning of them to be implemented by creating a good reason to shift to this platform from the traditional ones. That is by increasing profits of each of our consumers.

Based on needs some solutions were found.

First of all we needed a basic platform for establising a secure platform for future development hence a product was required which helps our consumers without us (or anyone) providing funds for the same. hence the first chapter of our book begins ( school ).

We also have various other projects in our mind, but as we have less manpower no almost no foundation to accomadate more developers, so we are currently building our base for it. Our idea is to provide platform were anyone will be able to contribute us a our own ( or even seperate if wanted) member in a manner that credits will remain to the original creator.

We needed it because most of the good solutions dont come out because of high cost of geting a patent or the idea might getting leaked.... But the major reasons involves unavailiblity of knowledge (which we want to eliminate with school , and a platform to listen to those creative ideas and give a good feedback.
